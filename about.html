Our History

From a small local shop to Bontoc's favorite footwear destination

2000

The Humble Beginning

KP Shoe Store opened its doors with a simple vision: to provide quality footwear to the Bontoc community. Our small shop quickly became known for personalized service and carefully selected products.

2005

Expanding Our Reach

As our reputation grew, we expanded our product line to include specialized athletic shoes for the growing number of fitness enthusiasts in our community.

2010

Community Partnership

We began sponsoring local sports teams and community events, strengthening our commitment to the people of Bontoc and Mountain Province.

2015

Store Expansion

Due to increasing demand, we expanded our physical store to double its original size, allowing us to offer an even wider selection of footwear.

2018

Modernizing Our Approach

We launched our online presence, allowing customers to browse and purchase our products from anywhere while maintaining our personal touch.

2023

Looking Ahead

Today, we continue to evolve while staying true to our roots, focusing on sustainable practices and expanding our range of eco-friendly footwear options.
